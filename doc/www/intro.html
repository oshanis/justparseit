Welcome to the Policy Parser!


Lawyers work with law-enforcement policies. Given a scenario and a set of policies, one of the laywer' main tasks is to determine whether a particular sequence of events in this scenario violates one or more policies. Due to recent advancement in knowledge-based reasoning, computer systems can now assist lawyers in this task. The AIR system is one of such systems.
